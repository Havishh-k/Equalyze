# DPDPA 2023 — Section 8: General obligations of Data Fiduciary

**Regulation:** Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA 2023)
**Jurisdiction:** India
**Article:** Section 8
**Domain Tags:** healthcare, fintech, hiring, insurance, all
**Effective Date:** 2023

## Text

A Data Fiduciary shall ensure the completeness, accuracy and consistency of personal data, where the personal data is processed to make a decision that affects the Data Principal.

A Data Fiduciary shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure effective observance of the provisions of this Act.

These measures must include safeguards against processing that may lead to discriminatory impacts, especially in automated decision-making systems.

## Compliance Requirements

- Personal data used for decisions must be accurate and consistent
- Technical measures (like bias auditing) must be implemented to ensure fairness
- Safeguards must prevent discriminatory impacts in automated decisions

## Violation Indicators

- Inaccurate data disproportionately affecting specific demographic groups
- Lack of technical measures (audits, fairness metrics) to detect bias
- Continued use of automated systems exhibiting demographic parity issues
